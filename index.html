Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to create a campaign?

Creating a campaign is completely free.

How long can my campaign run?

Campaigns can run for up to 90 days. You can extend if needed.

When will I receive the funds?

Funds are transferred to your account within 6-7 business days after your campaign ends.

Can I donate to multiple campaigns?

Yes! You can support as many campaigns as you'd like.

What happens if I don't reach my goal?

You can choose to either keep the funds raised or refund donors.
